About us

To Code or not to Code-that is the question most girls face today. Why go into a field dominated by men? Why be completely alone and isolated there? If there aren’t any women there..does that mean women can’t code? Entering a male dominated field is daunting beyond imagination.

At She Can Code, we've come up with an answer to this terrible conundrum. Here, we aspire to not only teach little girls how to code but , to provide them a community comprising of fellow girl programmers with whom they can share anything. A mentor will also be assigned to every girl, to foster a bond that transcends simple coding doubts to any problems or joys they're experiencing and wish to share.
Our aim is not only to reach girls how to code, but, to inspire confidence in them so that they may CODE WITH CONFIDENCE.
