Mentors

At She Can Code, we plan to assign a 15-17 year old Mentor to every 3 girls. The Mentor will be adept at C++ and will help guide the Mentees through learning C++. As an older and mature person, the Mentor will also always be at hand to provide emotional support and help to the Mentees.
